# Out-of-Line Behavior

## Overview
**Purpose:** Individual soldiers breaking formation to press advantage after successful combat
**Scope:** Confidence-based formation breaking, triggered by kills and high morale

## Status
**This mechanic is a subsystem of formations and will be designed in detail later.**

## Initial Concept

Confident fighters (high morale after kills) have probabilistic chance to:
- Break from formation line
- Press forward aggressively
- Create realistic chaos in battle lines

**Key factors:**
- Triggered after kill (morale surge)
- Probabilistic, not deterministic
- Creates visual realism (strongest fighters push ahead)
- Should be exception, not norm

## Dependencies
- Formation system (parent mechanic)
- Morale system (trigger condition)
- Combat stances (switches to aggressive)

## To Be Designed
- Exact trigger conditions
- Probability calculations
- How to rejoin formation
- Impact on formation cohesion
- Visual representation
